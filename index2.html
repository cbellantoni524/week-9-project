DSLR Cameras

DSLR cameras are preferable for those that are considered hybrid shooters, meaning they shoot vdieo and photography. They can be mirrored or mirroless and come in a pretty small form factor. They can be in a full frame which is equivelant to a 35mm film stock, or a crop sensor meaning a smaller sensor size with which is less senstive than full frame.
