Get To Know Cameras

This page site is here to educate people about the two most common types of cameras, DSLR and Cinema. Millions of people watch content on social media sites and try to become content creators but not many people know the difference between what makes some content look better than others.


DSLR cameras are digital single lens reflex cameras. These are the cameras that suceeded the typical home 35mm film cameras. These have take on a new evolution in the form of mirroless cameras which do not use any glass in the body and let light directly onto a censor. These have become known as hybrid shooters which means they are good for video and photo but mostly desirable for photos. These have become a staple for vloggers.

Cinema Cameras have one purpose which is to shoot video. These cameras have better sensors, have more dynamic rage for colors and shadows and have special settings to dial in specific shutter settings special for video.
